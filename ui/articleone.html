About



VIT University is a leading International Research University with campuses at Vellore and Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It has nine schools and is frequently ranked among the top technology research universities in India. Founded in 1984, as Vellore Engineering College, the institute was upgraded to a university in 2001 in recognition of its academic excellence. VIT's student strength has crossed 25000 with facu1ty complement of above 1100. It offers 18 undergraduate and 35 postgraduate programmes.
